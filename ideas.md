# Ideas & Roadmap

A living document of feature ideas, enhancements, and future directions for **Listen, Did You Know?**

---

## Near-Term (Next Sprint)

- **Scale to full channel** — Run the scraping pipeline against all 1,509 Shorts to grow the database from 76 to 1,000+ facts. The pipeline is already built; it's just a matter of running it.
- **Scheduled weekly scraping** — Automatically detect and add new Shorts as Justin posts them, keeping the database evergreen without manual intervention.
- **Improved share card** — Generate a custom Open Graph image for each fact so shared links render a rich preview card on Twitter/X, iMessage, and Slack.

---

## Medium-Term

- **Email opt-in** — Let fans subscribe to receive the daily "Listen, did you know..." fact directly in their inbox each morning.
- **Push notifications** — Progressive Web App (PWA) support with push notification opt-in for the daily fact.
- **Fact categories** — Tag facts by topic (science, history, food, sports, etc.) and let users filter the archive by category.
- **Merch store integration** — Wire up the Merch nav item to Justin's actual merchandise store when it launches.
- **Favorites** — Allow logged-in users to bookmark their favorite facts and revisit them in a personal collection.

---

## Long-Term / Big Ideas

- **"Fact of the Week" social card** — Auto-generate a shareable image each week featuring the most-shared fact, formatted for Instagram/TikTok.
- **AI "Did You Know?" generator** — A conversational interface grounded in all of Justin's facts, letting fans ask questions and get answers in Justin's voice.
- **Timestamp deep-links** — Link directly to the exact second in each Short where Justin says "Listen, did you know..." rather than just the video start.
- **Embed widget** — A lightweight embeddable widget that other sites can use to display a random Justin fact with a link back to the app.
- **Multi-creator expansion** — Extend the platform to support multiple creators, each with their own fact database and branded page.
